Petroleum Engineering

Capstone Project

Developed a testing procedure capable of measuring radial conductivity in a simulated borehole under pressure and temperature.
The testing procedure resulted in a 95.64% flowback for the first breaker, 86.38% flowback for the second breaker, and 110.25% flowback for the third breaker.
The first breaker showed a radial conductivity return of 0.556%, and the second breaker showed 0.009%, data for the third test was compromised either because of a leak or because the filter cake was not built up properly.
